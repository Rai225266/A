Project Requirements: Jan-Setu AI
​1. Project Overview
​Team Name: Setu Coders  
​Team Leader: Sourabh Rai  
​Problem Statement: [Student Track] AI for Communities, Access & Public Impact  
​Concept: Jan-Setu AI is a voice-first, multilingual bridge connecting rural communities to essential public services.  
​2. Problem Analysis
​The Problem: Millions of Indians are excluded from government benefits because of complex English/Hindi jargon and digital illiteracy.  
​The Goal: To bridge the "Information Gap" for rural citizens who cannot read or write in English/Hindi to access government benefits.  
​Community Impact: Directly aids the 25% of the population with low literacy.  
​3. Functional Requirements
​Voice-First Interaction: The system must remove the literacy barrier entirely by focusing on speech rather than text.  
​Multilingual Support: Integration with Bhashini for real-time translation into 12+ Indian languages.  
​Document Explanation: Users must be able to snap a photo of a government notice to hear it explained in their mother tongue.  
​Offline Support: Basic query resolution must be available via SMS for regions with poor internet.  
​Safety: Use of AWS Guardrails to ensure no misinformation is provided to citizens
